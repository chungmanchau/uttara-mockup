Services

Middleware and service integration solutions

Implementing and maintaining middleware solutions. Provide consultancy support for transforming legacy systems into Service oriented architecture. We do system study to understand the architecture and then come up with proposal to migrate the system in a phased manner. We help our customers at each stage of the migration process. Splitting up a monolithic application into several reusable services not only help our clients to save on application maintenance costs but also and makes it easier to extend on complex orchestration using business process flows.

Consultancy on Micro services

Its possible to best scale an application without the painstaking process of having to work on it for several months or years if it has the right architecture to begin with. Micro services makes it easy to scale. With a self contained focussed application service, spawning more instances in an elastic manner makes it that much easier. We have experience working for leading firms implementing such scalable architecture across high volume applications.

Search technologies

Having content is just one part of the puzzle and it loses value if it cannot be effectively served to the consumers of the content. In this digital era, ability to be able to search, filter and synthesise content provides the key for any large media companies to succeed. We offer consultancy in implementing solutions using search technologies to build complex search queries to be able to reach and get the right content.

Agile methodology consultancy

We practice and preach Agile methodologies and have seen success working with various partners and help them to embrace this methodology seamlessly. We help companies champion this highly successful development methodology and help them save time and especially help them avoid some common pitfalls.
